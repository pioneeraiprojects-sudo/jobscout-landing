Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 09, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Proposal Writer for Upwork (the "Extension") handles user data.

1. Data Collection

The Extension collects and stores your professional profile information (Bio) locally on your device using Chrome Storage. This data is used solely to personalize the AI-generated proposals.

2. Data Processing

When you click "Generate Proposal", the Extension reads the job description of the current Upwork tab and sends it along with your stored Bio to OpenAI's API. This is necessary to generate the response. No other data is transmitted.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We only share data with OpenAI (the service provider) to provide the core functionality of the Extension.

4. Security

Your Bio is stored locally in your browser. We recommend keeping your browser updated for maximum security.
